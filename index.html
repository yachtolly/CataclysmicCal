📜 Mayan Calendar Cheat Sheet

🔮 Tzolk’in (260-day ritual calendar)
• 20 day-names × 13 numbers → 260 unique days.
• Used for divination, ceremonies, and agriculture.
☀️ Haab’ (365-day solar calendar)
• 18 months of 20 days + 5 “nameless” days → 365.
• Civil calendar, tracking the solar year.
🔄 Calendar Round (52 years)
• Every 52 Haab’ years, the Tzolk’in & Haab’ align → big reset.
🗿 Long Count
• Tracks time from mythical creation date: August 11, 3114 BCE.
• Units: Kin = 1 day, Uinal = 20 days, Tun = 360, Katun = 7,200 (~20 years), Baktun = 144,000 (~394 years)
🤔 2012?
• Just the end of the 13th baktun — a cycle reset, not apocalypse.

☄️ Documented & Theorized Cataclysms

  • ~29,000 BCE — Hudson Bay pole shift — mass extinctions, ice sheets advance
  • ~18,500 BCE — Atlantis? Sudden flooding, Younger Dryas begins
  • ~11,500 BCE — End of Ice Age, massive floods, mammoths frozen
  • ~7,000 BCE — Noah’s Flood, Black Sea deluge, sea levels rise
  • ~3,000 BCE — Sumerian flood legend, drought & collapse
  • ~1,200 BCE — Bronze Age Collapse, earthquakes, migrations
  • 536 CE — “Year Without a Sun” — volcanic winter, famine
  • 1815 CE — Tambora eruption, “Year Without a Summer”
